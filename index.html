Thank you!

Hello there, we are Alex & Marie!

We want to say thank you for taking the time to learn about our family. We cannot imagine what has brought you to this moment, but we marvel at the strength and courage you have to pursue an adoption plan. We are so grateful that you have been led to us!

We know what it is like to have life go differently than planned. From the time we were married, we knew we wanted to be parents. In our seven years of marriage, we have lost multiple babies to miscarriage, but that has led us to God’s greater plan for us: adoption. We know that God’s will is for our family to be built in another way and we could not be more excited for this journey!

We believe that our choice to grow our family through adoption is purposeful and not accidental. We believe that our purpose in this life is to be parents. We hope we get the opportunity to know you. We want you to know that we will tell your baby of your bravery and love for them and we promise to honor you as we raise them. Know that we pray daily for your well-being and happiness and cannot wait to share the love we have for this baby with you.

With love,

Alex & Marie Thomas
