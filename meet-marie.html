Meet Marie

A few of my favorite things are crocheting, sewing, and using my Brother Scan ‘N Cut to make all kinds of fun projects. I could craft everyday and never get sick of it. I also love to play Just Dance and Beat Saber for hours. I love to cook and try new recipes. Desserts of all kinds are my usual go tos.

I love Harry Potter everything and I live for quiet nights at home with family or friends playing games. If there is one thing that I am 100% sure about myself, it is that I love people and thoroughly enjoy having them in my home and getting to know them. I love to entertain and cook for others, and my idea of a happy house is a full one.

I am a dog lover and absolutely adore our two fur babies, Athena and Apollo. I love to take them for long walks everyday and spoil them all I can. They look forward to playing with neighborhood kids or our nieces and nephews. They are so good with kids and I can’t wait to see them with our own children.

For as long as I can remember, when people would ask me what I wanted to be when I grow up my answer was always a mom. I feel that being a mother is my purpose in life and cannot think of a better thing I could be. I couldn’t be more excited to start the adoption process!

Marie's Family:

I grew up in a family of 7. I have two brothers and two sisters, who truly are my best and closest friends. I don’t think I can go a day without talking to my mom and sisters! My parents are my heroes, and they taught my siblings and I everything about hard work, service, and putting the Lord first in our lives.

Some of my favorite memories growing up are hiking and camping in the mountains, doing crafts with mom, visiting people with my dad, and staying up late talking with my sisters. I always looked forward to our summer road trips out to Michigan to visit my dad’s family because my parents made the journey fun and we got to spend a few weeks with his family.

I can’t wait to watch my siblings kids and our kids grow up together and take part in all the fun we have as a family.
