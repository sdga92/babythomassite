Meet Athena

Athena is a 3 year old Shorkie Poo. She is full of energy and love. She loves to give kisses and gets so excited when people come to visit, especially kids. She is very smart and learns tricks quickly. Athena does not like to get dirty or go outside when it is wet. She gets so excited when we get her clothes out and does not like when we let other dogs wear her sweaters.

Meet Apollo

Apollo is a 3 year old Shihtzu. He is the most cuddly puppy ever. He is very calm and loving. If you pet him you are his new best friend and he will stay with you until you stop. However, if you stop before he his done being pet he will paw at your hand or stick his head in your hand to let you know he wants more. He loves to go on walks and be outside, especially in the summer.

Apollo and Athena's Babies:

When Athena and Apollo were two years old we decided we wanted to breed them to have puppies. They had 3 boys and two girls (one of the girls didn't make it). It was amazing to watch the whole process and see Athena as a mom. She loved those puppies so much and loved to show them off to everyone who came to visit. We had Ellie for the first 9 months and Athena took care of her the whole time. My sister Liisa got Kofi, but her and her husband went to Taiwan for 6 months, so we watched Kofi while they were gone and Athena was so happy to have her baby back (especially because it was the same time Ellie was going to her home). We gave Atlas to my youngest sister, so we get to see him often too. King went to some friends of ours that live in Texas and we dog sit him and have play dates as well. Having puppies was so much fun and those puppies were the cutest things ever! We are so glad they went to family and friends so we can still see them.
