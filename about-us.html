Our Story

Told by Marie

Alex and I met when I was a freshman in high school. We had a class together and Alex liked me from the moment we met. I thought he was really cute and we went on a couple dates, but we never dated in high school. We did go to Homecoming together once! He was one of my best friends through high school, one who I could always count on, which would later serve us greatly in our marriage. My senior year, I studied abroad in France and he went on a mission for our church to South Korea.

We wrote back and forth and I knew he would be the one I’d marry. When he got home from his mission, we started dating. We loved to go hiking, spend time outdoors, play games, and spend time with our families. We were inseparable and it didn’t take long before we were engaged. We were married December 10, 2011 in Salt Lake City, Utah.

Our marriage and family values are based off of selfless service to each other, trust, unconditional love, patience, respect, and care and concern for the happiness and well-being of each other. We often talk about how grateful we are to have the kind of love and relationship we have and we can’t wait to share those values with our children.
